Why I Built This?

As an international student at Seattle Central College, I know how overwhelming it can be to start a new life in a different country.

What made it harder was the fact that important information was scattered across multiple websites and platforms. As a newcomer, it’s hard to even know where to begin.

That’s why I created this website — a simple, student-made guide built from my own experiences. It’s everything I wish I had when I first arrived: helpful resources, practical tips, and honest reflections.

I hope this site serves as a small bridge to help you feel more prepared, supported, and less alone. You're not the only one on this journey — and I’m rooting for you.

Just a heads-up:
Everything here is based on what I’ve personally found useful as a student. I’m not an expert, and things might change or vary — so make sure to verify things on official sites when needed!
